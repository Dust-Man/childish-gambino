Who is Childish Gambino?

Childish Gambino is the stage name of Donald Glover, an American actor, writer, comedian, musician, and producer. Born on September 25, 1983, in Edwards Air Force Base, California, Donald Glover adopted the stage name Childish Gambino for his music career.

As Childish Gambino, he gained widespread recognition for his unique blend of hip-hop, R&B, and funk music. Some of his popular albums include "Camp" (2011), "Because the Internet" (2013), and "Awaken, My Love!" (2016). Glover's musical work earned him several Grammy Awards, and his music often explores themes of identity, race, and culture.
